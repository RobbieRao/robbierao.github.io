Rao Fenggui (Robbie Rao) — Designer, Researcher, Founder

PhD student at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University School of Design (Asian Ergonomics Design Lab), China Academy of Art-trained designer, founder and lead of DesignAnything Lab. Research bridging generative AI, art-design workflows, HCI, public culture and creative education. Email robbie.rao@connect.polyu.hk. Selected work: ContextCam (CHI 2024), Flowing Ink Resonator, CityCure, DIVA framework, Let My Goldfish Go. See the terminal CV, academic CV and publications.
